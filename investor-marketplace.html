★ The Smarter Way to Invest

Build to Rent vs. Buying a Rental Property

When you buy a rental property from a national builder's investor marketplace — or any finished home off the MLS — you're paying for their profit margin, their sales and marketing costs, their carrying costs, and the risk they took building it on spec. All of that gets baked into the price you pay.

With Brightview's build-to-rent model, you own the land from the start. We're your general contractor — not the seller. There's no double closing, no title transfer markup, and no speculative builder margin stacked on top. You get the deal at true construction cost, and you walk away with tens of thousands of dollars in equity from day one.
