★ Winter Haven, FL overview

About building new in Winter Haven

Winter Haven is anchored by the 25-lake Chain of Lakes and downtown's Central Park, a lakefront centerpiece that's driven the city's downtown revitalization. LEGOLAND Florida brings 2+ million visitors annually, feeding the service economy, and AdventHealth's growing Winter Haven campus has made healthcare a major employer. Brightview Homes builds scattered-lot homes here in the $350K–$500K range — some of the most competitive new-construction pricing in Central Florida.

Why buyers are choosing Winter Haven

  • Chain of Lakes — 25 connected lakes with boating, waterfront living, and world-class bass fishing.

★ Location & commute

Getting around from Winter Haven

Winter Haven sits just south of I-4, giving 50-minute access to downtown Orlando and 45 minutes to Tampa. Disney is 40 minutes north, Orlando International Airport is 55 minutes northeast, and Tampa International Airport is 60 minutes west.

Schools

Polk County Public Schools — Winter Haven High School, Lake Region High School, and Winter Haven Senior Ninth Grade Center serve the city. Chain of Lakes Collegiate High School is a popular dual-enrollment option.
